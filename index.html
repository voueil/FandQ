Everything you need to know before booking your craft experience

1. What is Herafona?

Herafona is a cultural marketplace connecting tourists and locals with Saudi artisans. You can book live workshops or buy handmade crafts.

2. How can I book a craft workshop?

Visit our homepage, choose a craft (e.g., pottery, embroidery, weaving), select the artisan, pick a time, and complete your booking online.

3. Are the workshops beginner-friendly?

Yes! Our workshops are designed for all levels. Artisans will guide you step-by-step through the process, making it enjoyable even for beginners.

4. Can I buy products without attending a class?

Absolutely. Herafona offers both physical crafts and bookable experiences. You can explore products by category or artisan.

5. What areas are covered?

We work with artisans from across Saudi Arabia, including Riyadh, Jeddah, Al-Ahsa, Najran, and more. Explore hidden cultural gems!

6. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us through our chatbot or email us at support@herafona.com.

7. Can I suggest an artisan or craft to be added?

Yes! We welcome your suggestions. Please fill out our recommendation form or contact us directly.
